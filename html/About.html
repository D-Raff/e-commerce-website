Who We Are

Wolfspeed is a company run by born and bred speed demons, racers, engineers and designers; All of whom work in unison to create a brand that is dedicated to excellence, customer satisfaction, precision and most of all, performance.

We take pride in our ability to deliver quick and efficient results without a compromise in quality. Some of our biggest design projects are displayed on our backgrounds as verification of our skills as a Design House.

Why We Do This

We wanted to be the first workshop in South Africa to provide a service for every need in the car community; everything from scheduled servicing and detailing to building full race cars meeting the customer's every demand.

We also noticed that most racecars have to sacifice in looks or comfort for performance, we aim to build cars with a balance betweeen all 3, to allow our cars to be road-legal yet race-ready.
